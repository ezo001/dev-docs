How I work

  1. Discover

    Interview SMEs, read tickets and configs, and map the real workflow — not the idealized one.

  2. Structure

    Choose the right doc type (tutorial, reference, runbook, concept) and information architecture for the audience.

  3. Draft & test

    Write in the team's source format, validate steps in a staging environment, and iterate with reviewers.

  4. Ship & maintain

    Publish through CI/CD, track review flags, and plan release-time updates so docs don't rot.
